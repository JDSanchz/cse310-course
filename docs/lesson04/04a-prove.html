Language Module #1 - Plan

Prior to implementing your first discovery module over the next two weeks, you need to develop a plan. This plan needs to be completed as quickly as possible. You should not wait for a grade before starting work on the module.

  1. Download the language module #1 plan and results document.

  2. Complete the plan section. You should read the details of your selected module before completing the plan. The result section should be left blank. The result section will be completed when you submit your module in 05-Prove.

    • Select the discovery module that you will complete.

    • Rank your knowledge in this specific area

    • Identify the risks and your plans to overcome those risks.

    • Create a schedule for yourself to complete the module.

  3. Submit your plan and immediately begin work on the module.

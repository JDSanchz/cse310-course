W04 Teach: Team Project

Team Meeting

You need to meet together as a team at least once this week. Your recommended agenda is as follows:

  1. Opening prayer.

  2. Agree on a proposed project and complete the project plan together.

  3. Identify what needs to be researched and make assignments to team members. It is recommended that team members write code (proof of concept) to demonstrate the results of their research. While not required, it is recommended that code written as part of this research be put in the individual's personal software repository within GitHub with documentation for future use.

You may need to refer back to the Team Project description in W03 Teach.

Project Plan

Instructions

  1. Download the Project Plan template.

  2. Provide a detailed description of your proposed project, including specific requirements that the software will fulfill.

  3. Identify a high-level schedule for your project, including researching, implementing features, and testing.

  4. Identify risks that the team will need to resolve together early in the project.

You may start work on your project after submitting your project plan. However, you may be asked by your teacher to modify your proposed project. You should respond to feedback from your teacher quickly to get an approved project plan.

Rubric

This is both a team grade and a pass/fail grade. If the project plan is incomplete or lacking detail, then a fail will be given.

Submission

Your team needs to submit one copy of the following for this assignment:

  • cse310_project_plan.docx

Only one team member needs to submit a copy of the project plan.

Finding a Job: Me in 30 Seconds

During the reading from the SRS manual “Finding a Better Job,” you completed an activity where you wrote a “Me in 30 Seconds” and shared it with others to obtain feedback. Your assignment this week is to update your "Me in 30 Seconds" and practice it with a friend or family member.

  1. Download the cse310_me_in_30seconds.docx template.

  2. Write your updated “Me in 30 Seconds” based on the one you completed in the reading activity.

  3. Provide the name of the person that you practiced the “Me in 30 Seconds” with.

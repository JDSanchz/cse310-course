CS 246 | Software Design and Development

W06 Teach: Team Project

Team Meeting

You need to meet at least once together as a team this week. Your recommended agenda should be as follows:

  1. Opening prayer.

  2. Each team member should report on their assignments.

  3. Make assignments for team members to continue implementing different parts of the software. You should refer to the schedule that your team wrote in the project plan.

  4. Discuss technical risks and team concerns together and make plans to resolve them.

  5. Prepare the status report together.

You may need to refer back to the Team Project Description.

Project Status #1

Instructions

  1. Download the cs246_project_status_1.docx.

  2. Identify which parts of the software are completed, in progress, or not started.

  3. Identify risks that have been resolved or remain open. Identify open risks for any part of the software that you are behind schedule implementing.

  4. Show pictures or video of the software running in its current state.

Rubric

This is both a team grade and a pass/fail grade. If the status report is incomplete or lacking detail, then a fail will be given.

Submission

Your team needs to submit one copy of the following for this assignment:

  • cs246_project_status_1.docx

Only one team member needs to submit a copy of the status report.

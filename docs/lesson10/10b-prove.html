Finding a Job: Interviewing—Part 1

During the reading from the SRS “Finding a Better Job” manual, you completed activities that anticipated your responses to interview questions. Your assignment is to provide well-thought-out answers to these questions.

  1. Download the template cs246_interviewing_part1.docx.

  2. Answer the question: “What is one your strengths?”

  3. Answer the question: “What is your greatest professional or educational achievement?”

  4. Answer the question: “Why should we hire you?”

  5. Identify a potential question that you may be asked that you can answer with one of the power statements that you wrote. Provide both the potential question and your answer.

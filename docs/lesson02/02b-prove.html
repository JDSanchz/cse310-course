Finding a Job: Networking

During the reading from the SRS manual “Find a Better Job,” you completed an activity where you made a list of potential contacts you could network with to find employment opportunities. Your assignment this week is to expand this list to 20 potential contacts.

  1. Download the cs246_networking.docx template.

  2. Starting with the list you created during the reading activity, create a list of 20 potential contacts you could network with to find employment opportunities. For each contact, include a description of who they are (for example: former co-worker, teacher, former youth leader, relative, friend already employed).

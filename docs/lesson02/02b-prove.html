Finding a Job: Networking

During the reading from the SRS manual "Finding a Better Job", you completed an activity where you made a list of potential contacts you could network with to find employment opportunties. Your assignment this week is to come up with a list of 20 potential contacts.

  1. Download the cse310_networking.docx template.

  2. Starting with the list you created during the reading activity, create a list of 20 potential contacts you could network with to find employment opportunties. For each contact, include a description of who they are (e.g. former co-worker, teacher, former youth leader, relative, friend already employed, etc).

Submission

You need to submit the following for this assignment:

  • cse310_networking.docx

Rubric

This is a PASS/FAIL assignment. If any part of the assignment is incomplete or lacking detail, then you will receive a FAIL.

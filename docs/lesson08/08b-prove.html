Finding a Job: Resumes

During the reading from the SRS manual “Finding a Better Job”, you completed activities to begin the process of writing a resume. Your assignment this week is to write a resume following the guidance and examples in the reading. There are additional examples available at the BYU-Idaho Career Center. Frequently, job seekers who are still in college without much employment experience will put their education first on the resume.

  1. There is no template for this assignment.

  2. Create a resume following one of the patterns shown in the reading or at the BYU-Idaho Career Center.

Submission

You need to submit the following for this assignment:

  • A copy of your resume

Rubric

This is a pass/fail assignment. If any part of the assignment is incomplete or lacking detail, then you will receive a fail.

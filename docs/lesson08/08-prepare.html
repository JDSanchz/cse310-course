Finding a Job: Resumes

The Self Reliance Services (SRS) "Find a Better Job" manual is intended for use in a group but we will use it individually. Activities and Discussions in the reading should be done individually.

This week, we should complete the following reading:

  • Chapter 5, Introduction, Pages 77-78

  • Chapter 5, Section 1 - Three Simple Principles, Page 79

  • Chapter 5, Section 2 - Resumes, Pages 79-85

    • In the first activity, we will need to first find an entry level or internship job that we are interested in. We can search on a website like GlassDoor, Indeed, or Handshake. We can find more resources at the BYU-Idaho Career Center.

    • In the second activity, share our accomplishments with two or more friends or family members and ask for feedback.

  • Chapter 5, Conclusion, Page 89

    • We should watch the video "Better Written Materials" referenced in this section.

Additionally, spend a few minutes looking at the resume resources at the BYU-Idaho Career Center.

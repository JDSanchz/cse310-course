13-Teach: Team Project

Team Meeting

You need to meet at least once together as a team this week. Your recommended agenda should be as follows:

  1. Opening Prayer

  2. Each team member should report on their assignments.

  3. Follow-up on assignments for team members to complete the project. Ensure that the README.md file is completed. The last day to work on the project is the last day of the semester. No formal delivery of the project is made. The teacher will directly access your public GitHub repository.

  4. Remind all team members to individually complete the Individual Project Report (14-Teach). This report is confidential and should not be shared with other team members. The report is due on the last day of the semester.

  5. Remind all team members to individually complete the Inventory and Goals Survey - Revisited (14-Prove). This survey is an opportunity to show your growth over the semester. The survey is due on the last day of the semester.

You may need to refer back to the Team Project Description in 03-Teach.

There is no team deliverable this week.

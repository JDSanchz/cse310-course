W03 Teach: Team Project

You have been placed into teams according to the method selected by your teacher. The Team Project Description provides the details of the team project.

Team Meeting

You need to meet together as a team at least once this week. Your recommended agenda is as follows:

  1. Opening prayer.

  2. Read together as a team the Team Project Description which contains the purpose, requirements, schedule, expectations, and rubric for the project.

  3. Complete the Team Contract together.

  4. Begin brainstorming a project proposal. You will submit your project proposal and plan next week.

Team Contract

Instructions

  1. Download cs246_team_contract.docx.

  2. As a team (and also individually) read the details of the project (described above) and read the expectations section of the team contract. Discuss the details of the project and the expectations together as a team.

Rubric

This is both a team grade and a pass/fail grade. If the contract is incomplete or lacking detail, then a fail will be given.

Submission

Your team needs to submit one copy of the following for this assignment:

  • cs246_team_contract.docx

Only one team member needs to submit a copy of the contract.
